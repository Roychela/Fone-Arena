Unboxing OnePlus 6T

OnePlus has finally officially unveiled the OnePlus 6T and it’s priced at $549 in the US, which is slightly more expensive than the outgoing OnePlus 6.

The India pricing of the OnePlus 6T will be announced on October 30 at a separate event. The OnePlus 6T comes with minor upgrades, including a larger display with a waterdrop style notch, an impressive 3,700mAh battery, and a rather fast in-display fingerprint sensorThanks to these innovative features, the OnePlus 6T is one of the best devices from the company till date, however, it isn’t without its shortcomings on paper.

The device lacks a headphone jack (Never Settle?) and this might be the reason why a number of prospective buyers may be drawn away from it. For those to whom the headphone jack doesn’t hold as much importance, the OnePlus 6T might be the device to go for. If you are in that camp, and are interested in what you’ll be getting when you purchase the OnePlus 6T, then you’ve come to the right place..
