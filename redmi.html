Unboxing the Redmi Note 7

Smartphones are no longer just for making calls or messaging, you can do a whole lot of things with your smartphone now. It is imperative to get a hold on its specifications before buying any mobile as we run most aspects of our daily lives through it. The new Redmi Note 7 sports a 6.3" (16 cm) IPS LCD display, having a screen resolution of 1080 x 2340 pixels, aspect ratio of 19.5:9 and pixel density of 409 PPI. It features a bezel-less display with waterdrop notch with a screen to body ratio of 81.21 percent. The device is protected by Corning Gorilla Glass v5 against minor tears and scratches. Its capacitive touchscreen supports multi-touch functionality i.e. it responds instantly even on the subtle taps.

Xiaomi Redmi Note 7 functions on Android v9.0 (Pie) operating system and is powered by Octa-core (2.2 GHz, Quad core, Kryo 260 + 1.8 GHz, Quad core, Kryo 260) processor and RAM of 3 GB, sitting on 64-bit architecture. Its Adreno 512 graphics ensures exceptional graphical experience while playing games. Besides this, the device comes with a Light sensor, Proximity sensor, Accelerometer, Compass, and Gyroscope. The phone is 8.1 mm slim & weighs 186 grams, have a fingerprint sensor.
